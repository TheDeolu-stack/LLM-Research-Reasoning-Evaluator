# Logic Puzzles Test Cases


## 1. Neighborhood Delivery Puzzle
A mailman delivers to Oak, Pine, Maple, and Elm. He can only visit two per day.
Rules:
- Oak must come before Pine
- Maple cannot be paired with Elm
- Day 1 delivery: Oak + Elm
Question: What are valid Day 2 delivery pairs?


---
## 2. Seating Arrangement
Five students A, B, C, D, E sit in a row.
Constraints:
- A is left of C
- D is not next to B
- E is at one of the ends
Task: List all valid seating orders.


---
## 3. Event Ordering
Events: X, Y, Z, W
Rules:
- X before Y
- Z cannot happen after Y
- W must happen after Z
Task: Provide all valid orderings.
